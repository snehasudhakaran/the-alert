MJF Lion Dr.S. Suresh Balaji

Founder and Chief Editor of THE ALERT

"Discover the world through our lens, where words weave wonders and stories take flight."

MJF Lion Dr.S. Suresh Balaji is the Founder and Chief Editor of THE ALERT magazine. On a mission to serve, we can do our Best as a LION. I joined Lionism in 2000 and my motive to render serve had increased in a vast manner.

As a part of Lions Club activities, I planned to release a magazine for our Lions to enable them to know the History of Lions Clubs International and the By-laws of Lions Clubs International, District Events, Club functions, Club Service activities, etc. We are encouraged by the comments of our Lion Leaders, Our magazine consists of Lions' birthdays and wedding days of our District 324J, important events of our District, Articles motivating our Lions in lionism, family, personal life and all, Indian Constitution Act, Cooking tips, jokes.Our Lion Ladies, family and kids are also impressed by our magazine. From the 11th issue we give the list of birthday and wedding anniversary dates of the whole month, for your record.

While the interest for reading books are going down in these days, the demand for this magazine induces us to add more and more subjects. Soon this magazine will be on your hands as e-book and on website, along with the previous issues. We are planning to circulate this magazine throughout our Multiple. We expect your valuable support to encourage to continue.
