"On a Mission to Serve"

Lions serve. It's that simple, and it has been since we first began in 1917. Our clubs are places where individuals join together to give their valuable time and effort to improving their communities, and the world.

As a sign of accomplishment releasing a monthly magazine named “The ALERT” Comprises and covers all aspects of Lions club the periodic events happening in all clubs along with the general social matters and comical sections which inspires all age group peoples.
